## 🛰️ SENSOR TRUTH
1. Optical: Use HLS S30 (GeoTIFF). It includes Band B02 (Blue) for water penetration.
   - Target: Blue Band anomalies (submerged wrecks), NOT NIR glint (surface boats).
   - B02 (458-523nm) penetrates water to ~15-25m depth.
   - B08 (NIR) does NOT penetrate water - only sees surface boats.
2. SAR: Uses ASF HyP3 RTC_Gamma0 (GeoTIFF, dB scale).
   - Physics: Water is black (low dB). Wrecks are bright white (corner reflector).
   - Threshold: Coherence > 0.6, VV/VH ratio > 1.2.
   - Descending passes (~23:30 UTC) are best - afternoon wind creates capillary waves.
3. Thermal: Landsat B10/B11.
   - Physics: Submerged steel = Cold-Sink (retains cold).
   - Threshold: z >= 2.5.
